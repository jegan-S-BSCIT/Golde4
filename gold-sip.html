GOLD (SIP) – SYSTEMATIC INVESTMENT PLAN

What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a small amount regularly — every week or month — instead of a large one-time investment.

Through SIP, you gradually purchase gold or silver in small quantities and build your wealth over time.

It is a simple, disciplined, and risk-balanced way to convert your savings into real, tangible assets.
